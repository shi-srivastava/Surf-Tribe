Who is it for?

  • Our cleanups are open to everybody.
  • If you are older than 16 you can just come around.
  • Children between 14 and 16 need the consent of the parents, if they come alone.
  • Children under the age of 14 have to be joined by an adult with the right to supervise the child.
  • Please read our terms and conditions carefully, before joining our cleanups.

What can you expect

  • Our cleanups last around an hour.
  • It starts for everybody at the meeting point, that you can find below.
  • We hand out bags and clean gloves to everybody.
  • We organize the team in the beginning to assure an effective cleanup.
  • After an hour at the beach we are getting together again at the meeting point.
  • We weight what we collected and take everything to the next containers, to divide the trash and put it where it belongs.

What to bring

  • Bring what you feel comfortable in.
  • Be prepared of the strong sun of Barcelona.
  • Wear cloths that can get dirty and comfortable shoes.
  • Parents have to bring material for their children, as our gloves are only adult sizes.
  • Children might also enjoy using material, such as sieves and buckets, that have to be provided by the supervising adult.
